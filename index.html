Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson being Michael Jackson

Michael Jackson was a multi-talented musical entertainer who enjoyed a chart-topping career both with the Jackson 5 and as a solo artist. He released one of the best-selling albums in history, 'Thriller,' in 1982, and had other number-one hits on 'Bad' and 'Off the Wall.' He died in 2009 at the age of 50 of a drug overdose.
